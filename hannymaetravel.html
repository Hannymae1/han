This is my recent travel adventure in Banuangan and this is a place that located in Barangay Malasay, Katipunan, Zamboanga del Norte. It is a corner situated in Purok 7,that is not frequently visited by many people because it is far and not yet known as a tourist spot destination. Typically, the people living here are members of the indigenous people called Subanen, also known as Subanon, referring to a native group in the Zamboanga Peninsula area.In Banuangan, you can find unique tranquility and beauty of nature that brings peace to the residents there, and of course, to you as well. Banuangan also offers expansive views of wide fields mountains and falls that provide breathtaking sights.
